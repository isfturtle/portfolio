About Ilona

Ilona Fleischer is a December 2014 graduate of the Universtiy of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with a BA in mathematics. She is originally from outside Washington DC, and currently lives in Carrboro, NC.

Ms. Fleischer is a life-long turtle owner and is very knowledgeable about turtles. Currently, she lives with a Russian Tortoise named Sasha, whom she has had for four years. When she visits her parents, she cares for an additional 7 turtles, which have been with her family for between ten and thirty-five years.

Ms. Fleischer is a current member of CVP Encore Winter Guard, a winter guard in the Carolina Visual Productions family, competing in Independent Senior class in the Atlantic Indoor Association circuit. In the past, Ms. Fleischer has also competed with Carolina Visual Prodictions, an Independent A class guard competing locally in the Atlantic Indoor Association circuit and nationally in WGI, and Thomas Jefferson High School Winter Guard, a Scholastic A class guard competing in the Atlantic Indoor Association Circuit. She has also competed with in the guard in the Thomas Jefferson Marching Colonials, and performed non-competitively with the University of North Carolina Marching Tar Heels.

Other activities and interests include singing, Finnish, animals (in addition to turtles), mathematical modeling, and photography.
